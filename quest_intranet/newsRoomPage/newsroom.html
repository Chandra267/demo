Leadership Notes

We have not laid off a single person due to downturns in Industry. Every day, there are news about layoffs by big tech giants as well as startups in India. The fear of recession, the advancements in AI technologies and automation, or something else the reasons can be many. My personal assessment is that tech companies went on a hiring spree during the pandemic as there was a sudden interest in digital transformation initiatives. It also ended up muddling the job market as jacked-up salaries and joining bonuses were offered to make the candidates join the company. Now the situation has taken a dramatic turn with the possibility of a slowdown.
